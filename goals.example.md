- <e.g. individual single stocks, sectoral/thematic funds, narrow/emerging-market bets,
  single commodities like silver, anything concentrated or illiquid>

## Principles for suggestions

1. Grow a diversified medium-risk core first.
2. Keep the high-risk sleeve bounded and diversified within itself.
3. No single holding should dominate (flag anything above ~20%).
4. Prefer low-cost, direct-growth funds.
5. Keep some liquidity as dry powder.
6. Long horizon = stay invested; favour SIPs over churn.
7. Tax-aware: prefer adding new money over selling.
8. Respect the monthly budget when sizing buys.
